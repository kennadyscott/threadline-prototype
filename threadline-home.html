Questions, answered

The things people ask before they start.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT gives you an answer when you ask. Threadline is built around your brand — it remembers your voice, pillars, and audience, then carries an idea all the way from a half-formed thought to drafted pieces on a scheduled calendar. It's a workflow, not a chat box.

Is my content and data mine?

Yes. Everything you create is yours. Your brand, ideas, and drafts belong to you and can be exported or deleted at any time. We don't sell your data or train public models on it.

What does "build free, pay to activate" mean?

You can build your entire brand foundation and plan your content for free — no card required. You only pay when you're ready to activate: unlock scheduling, publishing, and higher AI limits.

Do I need my own AI keys or subscriptions?

No. Managed AI is included in every plan — Threadline runs the models for you, so there’s nothing to set up or connect. Each plan includes a monthly allowance of AI actions; heavier plans include more.

Can I really cancel anytime?

Anytime, in two clicks from Settings. No contracts, no cancellation calls. If you cancel, your account drops to the free build stage and your work stays intact.

Does it publish to my social accounts?

Yes — connect Instagram, LinkedIn, X, TikTok and more, and publish or schedule straight from your plan. Real post analytics flow back in so you can see what's working.
